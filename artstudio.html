About Art Studio

I'm Akbarkhuja Turakhujaev, an ambitious artist who finds joy and inspiration in the vibrant colors and endless possibilities of drawing and painting. Art has been a beloved hobby of mine for as long as I can remember.

Art for me is not just a pastime; it's a form of meditation, a way to unwind and find solace in a chaotic world. It's a delight to share my work with others, to evoke emotions or perhaps spark a connection through the visual stories I tell. Thank you for joining me on this artistic voyage. I hope my creations resonate with you as much as they do with me, and that they bring a splash of color and joy into your life. :^

Kabah

The Kaaba, often spelled as "Kabah" or "Ka'bah," is a sacred structure located at the center of Islam's most important mosque, Al-Masjid al-Haram, in Mecca, Saudi Arabia. It is a cuboid-shaped building made of granite masonry and stands as a focal point for Muslims around the world during their prayers.

Black Rose

The black rose is a symbol that has captured the imagination and emotions of people throughout history. While roses come in various colors, including red, yellow, white, and pink, the concept of a black rose is often associated with mystery, the unknown, and unconventional beauty. However, it's essential to note that a natural black rose does not exist. Typically, roses that appear black are actually a very deep shade of red, purple, or maroon.
